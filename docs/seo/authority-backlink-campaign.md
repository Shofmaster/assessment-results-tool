# Authority and Backlink Campaign (30 Days)

## Goal

Acquire 5-10 relevant, legitimate backlinks from aviation, aerospace quality, and compliance-adjacent websites.

## Target source categories

1. Aviation associations and member directories.
2. MRO/maintenance partner pages and supplier listings.
3. Aviation newsletters and operational excellence blogs.
4. Podcast, webinar, and event recap pages.
5. Standards/compliance consultants open to partner resource references.

## Outreach assets to publish first

- `/faa-repair-station-audit-checklist`
- `/aviation-audit-readiness`
- `/aviation-compliance-kpis`

These are linkable educational assets that support outreach without hard sales framing.

## Outreach script (email skeleton)

Subject: Resource for aviation quality teams

Hello {{name}},

We published a practical resource for aviation compliance teams:
{{url}}

It covers {{topic}} with evidence-focused workflows and can support your audience.
If you maintain a resources page for {{audience_type}}, would you consider including it?

Thanks,
{{sender_name}}

## Weekly cadence

- Week 1: Build target list of 40 prospects.
- Week 2: Send first outreach wave (20).
- Week 3: Send second outreach wave (20) and first follow-up for wave 1.
- Week 4: Follow-up for wave 2 and log wins.

## Reporting

Track in spreadsheet:

- Domain
- Contact name
- Date contacted
- Status (sent, replied, linked, declined)
- Linked URL
- Notes
